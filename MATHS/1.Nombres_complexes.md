- ### Racine n-ième

$$
\begin{align}
z^n &= Z \\
r^ne^{in\theta} &= Re^{i\varphi}
\end{align} \\
\left\{
\begin{array}{}
r &= \sqrt[n]R \\
n\theta &= \varphi + 2k\pi \iff\theta ={\varphi + 2k\pi\over n}
\end{array}
\right.
$$
