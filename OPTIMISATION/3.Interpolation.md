## **Interpolation**

------

### Interpolation de Lagrange

$$
P_n(x)=\sum\limits_{j=0}^{n} f(x_j)\cdot l_j(x)
$$

$$
l_j(x)=\prod\limits_{
\begin{align}
k=0 \\ k \neq j
\end{align}}
\frac{x-x_k}{x_j - x_k}
$$
